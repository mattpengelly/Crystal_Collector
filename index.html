How to Play Crystal Collector

The object of Crystal Collector is to click on the different crystals until your score is equal to the goal number.

Each crystal has a random value that you cannot see. When you click on a crystal, its value is added to your current score.

You must use your math skills to determine the values of the crystals and which ones to click to reach your goal.

Good Luck!!!
